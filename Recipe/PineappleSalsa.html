Pineapple Salsa

This fresh and vibrant Pineapple Salsa is a perfect accompaniment for tacos, grilled chicken, or fish. The sweetness of pineapple pairs beautifully with the heat of jalapeños and the freshness of cilantro.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh pineapple, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 jalapeño, seeded and finely chopped (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt to taste

Directions

Step 1

In a medium bowl, combine the diced pineapple, chopped red onion, and jalapeño.

Step 2

Add the chopped cilantro and lime juice. Season with salt to taste.

Step 3

Mix well and let the salsa sit for at least 15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

Step 4

Serve with tortilla chips or as a topping for your favorite dishes.

Chef's Notes

  • Feel free to add other ingredients like diced bell peppers or avocado for extra flavor and texture.
  • This salsa can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days, but it's best enjoyed fresh.
