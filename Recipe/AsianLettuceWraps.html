Asian Lettuce Wraps

These Asian Lettuce Wraps are a light, healthy, and flavorful dish filled with a savory mixture of ground meat and vegetables, served in crisp lettuce leaves.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground chicken or turkey
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup diced mushrooms
  • 1 cup diced bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 8-10 large lettuce leaves (e.g., iceberg or butter lettuce)
  • Chopped green onions and sesame seeds for garnish

Directions

Step 1: Cook the Meat

In a large skillet, heat vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the ground chicken and cook until browned, breaking it apart as it cooks.

Step 2: Add Vegetables

Stir in the mushrooms, bell pepper, onion, garlic, and ginger. Cook until the vegetables are tender, about 5-7 minutes.

Step 3: Add Sauce

Pour in the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, rice vinegar, and sesame oil. Stir well to combine and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.

Step 4: Assemble the Wraps

To serve, spoon the mixture into the center of a lettuce leaf, fold it up like a wrap, and garnish with chopped green onions and sesame seeds.

Chef's Notes

  • Feel free to customize the filling by adding more vegetables or swapping out the protein for tofu for a vegetarian option.
  • These wraps can be served as an appetizer or a light main dish.
