No-Fry Spicy Potato Skins

No-Fry Spicy Potato Skins are a healthier version of the classic appetizer, baked instead of fried, and topped with zesty spices, cheese, and your favorite toppings. Enjoy them as a snack or party treat!

Ingredients

  • 4 medium potatoes (russet works best)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or your choice)
  • 1/2 cup cooked bacon bits (optional)
  • Chopped green onions for garnish
  • Greek yogurt or sour cream for serving

Directions

Step 1: Prepare the Potatoes

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Wash the potatoes thoroughly, then pierce them with a fork several times. Bake them directly on the oven rack for about 45 minutes or until tender.

Step 2: Scoop Out the Flesh

Once the potatoes are cool enough to handle, cut them in half lengthwise. Scoop out most of the flesh, leaving a thin layer to maintain structure. Reserve the scooped potato for another use (like mashed potatoes).

Step 3: Season and Bake the Skins

Brush the potato skins with olive oil and season with smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Place them back in the oven and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until crispy.

Step 4: Add Toppings

Remove the skins from the oven and sprinkle with shredded cheese and bacon bits (if using). Return to the oven for another 5 minutes, or until the cheese is melted.

Step 5: Serve

Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot with Greek yogurt or sour cream on the side.

Chef's Notes

  • Feel free to customize the toppings with jalapeños, olives, or any other favorite ingredients.
  • For a vegetarian option, skip the bacon and add more veggies.
  • These potato skins can be made ahead of time and reheated in the oven before serving.
