Banana Chai Bread

This banana chai bread combines the sweetness of ripe bananas with the warming spices of chai for a comforting twist on the classic banana bread recipe. Perfect for breakfast or as a cozy afternoon snack with tea.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups mashed ripe bananas (about 3 medium bananas)
  • 1/3 cup melted coconut oil (or butter)
  • 1/2 cup honey or maple syrup
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 3/4 cups whole wheat or all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon ground chai spice (or substitute with 1 tsp cinnamon, 1/2 tsp cardamom, 1/4 tsp ginger, 1/4 tsp allspice)
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts or pecans (optional)

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan or line it with parchment paper.

Step 2

In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the mashed bananas, melted coconut oil, honey (or maple syrup), eggs, and vanilla extract until smooth and combined.

Step 3

Add baking soda, salt, chai spice, and flour to the wet ingredients. Stir until just combined. If using, fold in the chopped nuts.

Step 4

Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and spread it evenly. Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

Step 5

Let the bread cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then remove it from the pan and let it cool completely on a wire rack before slicing.

Step 6

Slice and serve with a cup of tea or coffee. Store leftovers in an airtight container for up to 5 days, or freeze for longer storage.

Chef's Notes

  • If you don't have chai spice, you can easily make your own blend with common spices like cinnamon, ginger, and cardamom.
  • This recipe works well with either honey or maple syrup for a natural sweetener.
  • For a vegan version, replace the eggs with flax eggs (1 tablespoon ground flaxseed + 2.5 tablespoons water per egg).
