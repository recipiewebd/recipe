Indian-Style Basmati Rice

This recipe provides perfectly fluffy and fragrant Indian-style Basmati rice, a perfect accompaniment to any curry or dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon butter or ghee (optional)

Directions

Step 1

Rinse the basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear. This removes excess starch and prevents the rice from being sticky.

Step 2

In a medium saucepan, combine the rinsed rice, water, and salt. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat.

Step 3

Once the water begins to boil, reduce the heat to low, cover the pan, and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until all the water is absorbed and the rice is tender.

Step 4

Remove the pan from heat and let the rice sit, covered, for 5 minutes to steam. This will make the rice fluffy.

Step 5

Fluff the rice with a fork and stir in butter or ghee if desired. Serve hot with your favorite curry or dish.

Chief's Notes

  • You can add whole spices like cinnamon, cloves, and cardamom to the water for added flavor.
  • If you prefer softer rice, add an extra 1/4 cup of water.
